About Aetherfall

Aetherfall is a city built upward from necessity, layered with steel, stone, and arcane energy. Corruption spreads, rebellion simmers, and every choice reshapes both the city and the people within it.

Characters

Zyra “Riot” Kade

Anarchist. Volatile. Unhinged. Her destructive amplification bends arcane energy into chaos. From Underfall streets to High Rises confrontation, Zyra embodies collapse itself.

Aelira Virex

Water-touched. Fragile yet powerful. In her mafia-boss form, she commands reality through contracts and dominion magic, balancing order against the chaos Zyra spreads.

The Layers of Aetherfall

  • The Underfall: Sunless, chaotic, and dangerous. Survival is brutal.
  • The Midstack: Working heart of the city. Influence matters more than strength.
  • The High Rises: Sealed towers where decisions ripple downward without resistance.
