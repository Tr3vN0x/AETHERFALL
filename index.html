Zyra "Riot" Kade

Anarchist. Volatile. Unhinged. She doesn't create magic—she overdrives it. Arcane energy reacts violently around her, surging faster and burning hotter.

Notes: Zyra doesn't want control; she wants collapse. Her influence is a virus on the Lattice.

Aelira Virex

Water-touched. Emotionally unstable. Her magic responds to emotion first. When she loses her center, the city begins to drown.

Notes: She survives her power; she does not dominate it.

Sylva Morren

Calculated. Absolute Authority. She treats the city like a chessboard where she already owns the king.

Notes: She is not chaotic; she is inevitable.
