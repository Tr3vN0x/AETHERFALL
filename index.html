— THE TRIAD OF POWER —

The Catalyst

Zyra “Riot” Kade

"She doesn't want control. She wants collapse."

Zyra stands with her weight forward, muscles defined from survival rather than training. One side of her hair is shaved close, the rest swept forward in a jagged crimson-black fall. Her eyes are sharp green and restless—scanning for threats.

Power: Destructive Amplification. She overdrives magic until it breaks containment. The closer she gets to chaos, the stronger she becomes.

The Dominion

Aelira Virex

"She doesn't dominate power. She survives it."

Aelira's presence is colder, heavier. Blue hair braided into twin plaits that fall like anchors. Pale blue markings glow across her skin like cracks in glass. Light bends near her; moisture gathers when she breathes.

Power: Fluid Manipulation. Water that behaves incorrectly. It moves without gravity and flows through solid matter, responding to her emotions first.

The Authority

Madame Sylva Morren

"She is not chaotic. She is inevitable."

Sylva is sharpened rather than fractured. Posture immaculate, chin lifted. Short platinum hair and a tailored coat of ivory and gold. Every symbol on her person represents a contract, a loyalty, or a consequence.

Power: Dominion Influence. She binds magic. Contracts seal when she speaks. People feel a heavy pressure in their chest when they lie to her.
