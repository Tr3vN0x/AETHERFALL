The Catalyst

Zyra “Riot” Kade

Anarchist. Volatile. Unhinged.

Zyra stands with her weight forward, always ready to lunge. Her build is lean but hardened, muscles defined from survival rather than training. One side of her hair is shaved close, the rest swept forward in a jagged crimson-black fall that looks deliberately aggressive.

She wears stripped-down combat gear: street armor scavenged from industrial zones. Her fists are always clenched, as if violence is a reflex she never learned to turn off. She doesn't want control. She wants collapse.

The Dominion

Aelira Virex

Water-touched. Dangerous without trying.

Aelira's presence feels colder, heavier. Her long blue hair is braided into thick twin plaits that fall like anchors over her shoulders. Pale blue markings glow faintly across her skin, branching like cracks in glass, pulsing softly as if responding to an unseen tide.

The Mafia Persona: Elegant. Calculated. Absolute authority. Tailored in ivory and gold, every symbol on her coat represents ownership. She is not chaotic. She is inevitable.
